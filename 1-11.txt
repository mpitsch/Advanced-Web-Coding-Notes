Bounce Rate - percentage of people who leave never having seen more than one page of your site

In general, when you retrieve an HMTL page, a request is sent for the page, and is sent to the requester
in packets, checked with a checksum and various algorithms to ensure the data is correct.

With a PHP file, a relevant database is searched, whatever information is needed is taken, it builds an
HTML file to return (though the file extension is still .php) to the client.
